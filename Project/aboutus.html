About Us

The BLOOD BANK MANAGEMENT SYSTEM is great project. this project is designed for successful completion of project on blood bank management system.

The basic building aim is to provide blood donation service to the city recently. Blood Bank Management System (BBMS) is a Web based application that is designed to store, process, retrieve and analyze information concerned with the administrative and inventory management of blood banks as well as individual donors.

This project aims at maintaining all the information pertaining to blood donors, different blood groups available in each blood bank and help them manage in a better way.
